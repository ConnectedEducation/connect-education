Participants

Students will learn the principles of search engines and information retrieval. Information filtering and retrieval drives some of the world's most successful and high-tech businesses. Students will learn various methods of search engine optimization (SEO), ranging from theory to implementation. Students will learn how to use tools and methods to perform searches and utilize results effectively. They will also learn how large data sources such as social media affects information retrieval.
